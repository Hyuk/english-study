```
1. In line with policy, all passengers must confirm reserved seats with the airline ------ the date of departure.
  a. prior to
  b. with respect to
  c. in spite of
  d. on account of

  * 모든 승객은 출발일 이전에 반드시 항공사에 예약된 좌석을 확인해야 한다.

  * in line with: ~에 따라
  * confirm: 확인하다
  * reserved: 예약된

  * prior to: ~이전에
  * with respect to: ~에 관하여
  * in spite of: ~에도 불구하고
  * on account of: ~때문에
```

```
2. A memorandum was sent to employees to inform them of a small ------ in their work timetable.
    1. modifying
    2. modified

    4. modify

```
